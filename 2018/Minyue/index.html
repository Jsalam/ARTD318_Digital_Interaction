Digital Interaction · Graphic Design· UIUC Juan Salamanca 2018 Empirical study of Fitts Law ... on grids All the files in this example are available at github Experiment design The aim of this experiment is to define the best distribution of 5 clickable elements on the screen. By 'best' we mean the arrangement that affords the shortest time to click on all the elements in the sequence defined by their IDs. You can define their position, their size and their shape (either a rectanlge or circle). Overlaping elements are not allowed!
